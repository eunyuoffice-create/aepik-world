### Ver.A - 모바일 메인 홈 (전체 화면)

```
A clean, modern mobile app UI design for "Aepik World", a fan goods exchange and group buying marketplace platform. iPhone mockup showing the full home screen.

Top area: minimal white header with the brand name "Aepik World" in blue (#3B82F6) bold sans-serif font, search bar with rounded corners and light gray background, notification bell icon.

Hero banner section: a soft gradient carousel banner (light blue #DBEAFE to medium blue #93C5FD to brand blue #3B82F6) with event announcement text and dot indicator at the bottom.

Content sections with white background: "Upcoming Events" horizontal scroll cards with small category color-coded tags (purple, pink, amber, cyan), "Popular Exchanges" 2-column product card grid with 1:1 square product images and category pill tags, "Group Buy" horizontal scroll cards with progress bars, "Wanted Posts" compact text list cards.

Bottom navigation bar with 5 equal tabs (Home, Exchange, Events, Chat, My Page) using thin line icons, active tab highlighted in blue.

Design style: ultra-clean white-based marketplace UI, minimal shadows, 12px rounded cards with thin gray borders, Pretendard/Inter sans-serif typography, generous whitespace. Trust-oriented C2C trading platform aesthetic similar to Kream, Bunjang, Karrot Market. No heavy decoration, content-first approach where product photos are the hero.

Color palette: primary blue #3B82F6, white #FFFFFF background, gray #F9FAFB sections, category accent colors - purple #8B5CF6 (anime/manga), pink #EC4899 (idol/artist), amber #F59E0B (actor/stage), cyan #06B6D4 (game).

High fidelity UI mockup, realistic mobile app design, professional, Dribbble quality, 4K resolution.
```

## 사용 팁

- **Midjourney**: `--ar 9:19` (모바일 비율) 또는 `--ar 16:9` (쇼케이스) 추가
- **DALL-E**: 프롬프트가 길면 핵심 키워드 위주로 줄여서 사용
- **Firefly / Ideogram**: 텍스트 렌더링이 필요하면 `with readable text "Aepik World"` 강조
- **추가 키워드**: `--style raw` (Midjourney), `app store screenshot quality`, `Figma design`, `UI kit`
